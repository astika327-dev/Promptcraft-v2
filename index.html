      FAQ

      Answers for prompt builders

      Still curious? Here are a few things teams ask before adopting PromptCraft.

      Where are my templates stored?

      Everything you build stays in your browser by default. Use Export to create a JSON backup or to sync with teammates.

      Can I restore the starter templates?

      Yes. Use “Restore default library” anytime to reset back to the original selection. Your custom additions will be removed.

      Do you collect any of my data?

      No tracking pixels here. PromptCraft runs completely client-side so your prompts, values, and templates never leave your device.

      How do I share a template with teammates?

      Click “Copy template JSON” from the preview panel. Send that snippet or the exported file to any teammate for instant import.
